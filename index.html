Sebastian's Favorite Foods

Sushi

Sushi is a Japanese dish with a variety of ingredients, such as seafood, often raw, and vegetables.

Nachos

Nachos are a Tex-Mex food consisting of fried tortilla chips or totopos covered with melted cheese or cheese sauce, as well as a variety of other toppings, often including meats, vegetables, and condiments such as salsa or sour cream.

Burgers

A hamburger is a food consisting of fillings —usually a patty of ground meat, typically beef—placed inside a sliced bun or bread roll.
